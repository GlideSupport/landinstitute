Pursuits

Incubate

We identify what is most needed and develop proofs of concept. We initiate, test, and validate perennial grains, while also investigating and engaging with the cultural ideas that shape perennial agriculture.

Guide

By leveraging our standing, we legitimize and support valuable research, while also sharing insights and resources to strengthen the capabilities of people and organizations. Building on this foundation, we design solutions and provide consultation for the real-world application of our work.

Cooperate

We recognize the urgent need for co-creation with those who can support and expand our capacity. Externally, we invest in cultivating mutually beneficial, long-lasting relationships, while internally, we honor all contributions to the movement and set aside personal egos to advance what is needed most.

Activate

We galvanize people to believe in a better future and actively engage them in creating it. To support this vision, we develop the market intelligence necessary to make perennial agriculture commonplace, and we tell accessible, relevant, and localized stories that inspire action from both the public and our patrons.
