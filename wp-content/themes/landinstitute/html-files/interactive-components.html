The Salina Regional Airport (SLN) has direct daily flights to and from Denver International and Chicago O’Hare airports through United. The two nearest major airports are located in Dwight D. Eisenhower National Airport (ICT) in Wichita, Kansas, about 90 minutes south of Salina on interstate I-135, and Kansas City International Airport (MCI), about 3 hours east of Salina on I-70. Manhattan Regional Airport (MHK), about 70 miles east of Salina on I-70, has several flights to major hubs, such as Chicago and Dallas. The prices are often comparable or beat those of Wichita.
